Recycling - you do it too!

How does the Return System work?

Bottles and cans with a deposit mark are disposable packaging that can be recycled, melted and turned into new bottles and cans.

Pant A = DKK 1.00 - glass bottles and aluminum cans less than 1 liter
Pant B = DKK 1.50 - plastic bottles less than 1 liter
Pant C = DKK 3.00 - all bottles and cans of 1–20 liter

Why should I recycle?

Every year, more and more one-way bottles and cans are sold, but only 89% had been returned. However, in 2019, 92% were returned for the first time. This extra one percentage point sounds small, but it represents more than 12 million more bottles and cans that can be recycled.

How do the machines work?

Reverse vending machines accept bottles and cans and scan the deposit mark, barcode and shape. The machine then calculates how much money to refund to you. The reverse vending machine feeds the bottles and cans into containers that are collected by Dansk Retursystem. The bottles and cans are then recycled, melted and turned into new bottles and cans.

I want to donate. Where does my money go?

Where the money gets donated to is up to each store, however it usually goes to people and families in need.

Are there any limitations as to where I can use the receipts?

The receipt usually will have the name of the store on it. You can use it in the specific store, the location doesn’t matter. For ex. If the receipt has the store name “Netto”, you can exchange it in all “Netto” stores all over Denmark.
